About Us

Developer & Designer

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Prajwal, and I'm a freelance web developer with over 5 years of experience in the industry. I specialize in creating custom websites and web applications that help businesses and individuals achieve their online goals. After completing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, I worked as a developer at a digital agency for several years. During this time, I had the opportunity to work on a variety of projects, ranging from small business websites to large-scale e-commerce platforms. In 2018, I decided to start my own freelance business, and I haven't looked back since. I love the freedom and flexibility that comes with being my own boss, and I'm passionate about helping my clients succeed online. My areas of expertise include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP, as well as various CMS platforms such as WordPress and Drupal. I'm also skilled in SEO and content strategy, which helps me create websites that are both beautiful and functional. When I'm not working on client projects, you can usually find me hiking or biking in the great outdoors. I'm also an avid reader and enjoy learning about new technologies and trends in my field. If you're interested in working with me, I'd love to hear from you! Feel free to contact me directly through my portfolio or connect with me on social media using the links below.
